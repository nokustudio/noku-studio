Of Stillness

A barstool by Noku Studio

Collection 01

Where form
meets quiet

A meditation on form and function. The barstool merges organic curves with structural precision — walnut meets linen in a conversation between strength and softness. Each joint is cut by hand, every curve refined through iteration.

Material Solid walnut
Upholstery Belgian linen
Joinery Mortise & tenon
Height 760 mm
Lead time 3–4 weeks
Materiality

Honest materials,
lasting presence

Walnut

Rich, dark grain with natural warmth. Ages gracefully, developing deeper character over decades of use.

Linen

Breathable natural fibre that softens with time. Hand-selected from European mills for superior drape and texture.

Brass

Patinas beautifully with age. Subtle accents that ground modern forms in tactile, precious materiality.

Good design is not about adding more. It is about honouring what remains when everything unnecessary has been removed.
