D4 · D5

The live site uses Josefin Sans at 400 for section headings — not the ultra-light 200-300 visible in our local dark-hero panels. Weight 200 is architecturally correct at 80px+ on a dark canvas (visual mass comes from scale). At 40-64px on a light background, 400 is the appropriate weight — it has presence without feeling heavy. Nav and labels always use 600 confirming Josefin Sans as the sole display AND UI typeface.

03 — Section Usage

Two registers. One palette.

Not a light/dark mode toggle — one palette used two ways. Which colour and type roles apply depends on the section background. Chamoisee leads headings in both registers; everything else flips for contrast.

Light section Featured · Eyebrow 600

A Quiet Confidence

Body copy is set in ink with Quattrocento for long-form legibility — it carries the reading weight so the warm chamoisee can stay reserved for headings.

Caption and metadata sit in muted — quieter, still AA-legible.

View the collection ↗
  • AA Large · 3.5:1--chamoisee for headings, links, eyebrows — large or 600 weight only. Never body copy on light.
  • AA · 13.6:1--ink body copy — the legibility workhorse on light surfaces.
  • AA · 5.5:1--muted captions, metadata, secondary text.
  • Surfaces--line dividers & borders. Backgrounds: --light base, --light-secondary cards, --cream warm zones.
Dark section Featured · Eyebrow 600

Of Stillness

On dark the body flips to bone — a warm off-white holding 12:1 contrast. Chamoisee gains contrast here too, so it can carry more than just headings.

Captions use bone at 60% — dimmed, still AA at 5.1:1.

View the collection ↗
  • AA · 4.6:1--chamoisee headings + highlights — passes body size on dark, vs large-only on light.
  • AA · 12.1:1--bone body copy — the light-on-dark workhorse.
  • AA · 5.1:1--bone at 60–70% opacity for captions & dimmed text.
  • Decorative--dark-border dividers (1.8:1 — lines only, never text). Grain overlay at 3.5% adds analog texture.

D9 · D10

All button borders use border-radius: 100vw (pill) matching the live site's --radius--round. The live site also uses 1rem (16px) cards and 0.5rem (8px) for small UI — our --radius-md and --radius-sm. Border width: 1px (--border-width). The live site specifies 1.5px main / 0.8px on buttons — we use 1px as a clean midpoint.

Refined set — proposed

Two shapes with intent — squared for the primary commerce action, pills for secondary and inline. Hierarchy comes from value (solid espresso → outline → text), not three near-identical tan pills. Every control now has hover, focus-visible and active states. Tab through to see the focus rings.

Primary actions use solid fills (espresso on light, bone on dark) — 10.8:1, full AA body. Chamoisee stays as outline/accent and label text, consistent with the brand's headings and eyebrows (AA-large). Dark outlines now use chamoisee (4.58:1) instead of the near-invisible --dark-border (1.82:1).
