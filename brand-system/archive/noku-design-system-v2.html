Buttons

Primary CTA · .buy-now-btn / .checkout-btn

Chamoisee fill → paper on hover. Used for primary commerce actions.

Transparent border → fills ink on hover. Used on product cards.

Used in material selectors and configurator. wood-swatch uses rounded border, materials-pill is sharp-edged.

Link-style CTAs. Collection link animates gap; view-all has chamoisee underline.
