Deodar House

Year 2023
Spaces Dining & Bedrooms
Collaborators Studio Lore
Brief A project where different materials were explored while considering the sensibilities of existing structures as well as the interiors chosen by the client. A common thread connected everything in a space that the furniture seamlessly tied into!
Concept

Each bed in the house had a different style. The guest bed had woven cane panels on the headrest and footboard, an ode to Mid-Century Modern, while the children's bed had simple planks on the headboard, a more utilitarian design. The master bed was our impressive half-poster bed with turned details on the legs and an upholstered headboard that appeared to float between the legs.

Solid Honne table was made to fit the open dining space that connected the living to the kitchen. The chairs were not upholstered to showcase the beauty of the wood.

Teak finish side tables with 2 drawers and grooved handles became a staple across all three bedrooms due to their simplicity.
