Temple Tree House

Year

2022

Spaces

Living, Bar & Bedrooms

Collaborators

Studio Lore

Brief

Elegance and luxury were the core principles of the Patterns B38 project. We aimed to capture these through the use of premium wood, brass, fabric, and leather, combined with a design that exuded subtle opulence. We incorporated repeating elements such as vertical grooves and Chesterfield details on the headboard to create continuity throughout the house while using colors to give each space its own personality.

Green leather of the barstools contrasted beautifully with teak wood, extending the home's ground-floor theme. A single curved wood piece formed the backrest, creating an instant focal point.

The master bed is one of our grandest pieces, with a large headboard featuring a central upholstered section and winged sides that wrap around the bed and side tables. Mounted on an extended frame, the side tables appear to float. Vertical lines add texture, while Chesterfield detailing enhances the luxury.

A darker blue bed upholstery was chosen for the daughter’s bedroom. This brought in colour while the dark tone worked well with the ample light from the bay window. As per client's wish, we added a seating/ storage on the footboard end to increase functionality.
