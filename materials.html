Material Honesty

At Noku Studio, we believe that materials should not be masked. Our finishes are designed to showcase, rather than hide, the organic beauty of the timber, the natural grain irregularities of the leather hides, and the detailed weave patterns of the linen fabrics.

Every log of teak, white ash, or honne wood is selected by hand at our workshop, seasoned patiently to survive generations of climates, and finished with natural oils that allow the wood to breathe and darken gracefully over time.
