Collection 02

Of Memories

Pieces from the past finding a place in the current. Indian furniture, born from the influences and inspirations of different regions and cultures, is re-explored in this collection. These pieces are designed with vernacular spaces in mind and evoke memories of homes that many generations have lived in and experienced.

Design Language

Vernacular & Traditional

Re-exploring local region aesthetics, combining structural memory with balanced traditional detailing, warmth, and craft-driven ornamentation.

Context

Residential & Heritage

Created specifically for domestic sanctuaries, quiet reading corners, central family dining halls, and warm, welcoming home entrances.

Material Palette

Nostalgic & Rich

Local premium Indian hardwoods (Honne, Bhilwara), intricate hand-woven cane details, deep Montana leather finishes, and solid brass chains or hardware.

Curation

Memories Moodboard

Warm spaces infused with the flourishing spirit of art and craft. Details developed through ages.
