Materiality

Teak

Tectona grandis

Renowned for its rich golden to medium brown hues that deepen with age, teak's straight grain and coarse texture exude timeless elegance. Naturally resistant to water, rot, and pests, its durability ensures lasting beauty with minimal upkeep.
