Materiality

The Soul of the Object

We believe a piece of furniture is only as honest as the materials from which it is born. At Noku Studio, we do not mask the organic. We celebrate it.

Our reclaimed teak is rescued from decades-old structures in Southern India—seasoned naturally by sun and monsoon, carrying a warm history and unparalleled stability.

Each strand of natural rattan cane is meticulously hand-woven by local master artisans, marrying breathing mid-century forms with India’s living heritage.

Our certified hides are left aniline-finished, preserving the natural grain, scars, and creases, designed to acquire a unique, rich patina with every touch.
